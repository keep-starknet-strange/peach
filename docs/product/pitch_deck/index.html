Why Starknet?

  • Speed: 1-2 seconds transaction confirmation time
  • Cost: Transfers cost less than $0.01

    • Settled over $1 trillion in value
    • In production for 4 years

How Venuela Works

  1. Event Creation: Organizers list events on Venuela
  2. Ticket Minting: Tickets are issued as digital assets on Starknet
  3. User Purchase: Buyers purchase tickets with a familiar, easy interface
  4. Secure Storage: Tickets are stored in users' digital wallets
  5. Event Entry: Scannable codes or NFC for quick, secure check-ins
